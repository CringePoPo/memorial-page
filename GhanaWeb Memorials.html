Vida Mamle Dugbartey TRIBUTE FROM SON-IN-LAW MR. LOUIS BRIGHT SEDEGAH SNR TRIBUTE TO LATE MOTHER-IN-LAW MAD.VIDA MAMLE DUGBARTEY A PIECE OF REMEMBRANCE: Truly, human beings are nothing but a puff of air and for that matter no one can buy life unending nor avoid coming to the grave. So lives are mist that appear for a little time and then vanish. Therefore, generally, life on this our mother earth is transient. All of our material possessions- riches and wealth gold and silver will fizzle away one day. Frankly Speaking, I felt a tiny pang of loneliness on hearing the sad news of my mother-in-law far away from mother land Ghana. My mother-in-law was a woman full of forethought, assiduity and expansive mind. She was somebody who hated pride, arrogance, evil behaviour and perverse speech. She took me as her own biological son rather than her son-in-law. This love therefore made me what I am today. From her, I received more additional home training. She taught me that the honours we might receive in this life would fade automatically into insignificance compared to the honour that God will confer on us. We should therefore follow God in all our endeavors closely. On any occasion that I returned from my journey very beautiful smile would animate her face and would be the first person to meet me and embraced me with welcoming hands. Such is the good mother-in-law I am going to miss for ever and ever’ What a Lovely mother-in-law you were My mother-in-law had no envy or hatred, she was full of love and charity, humility and sympathy. On all notes, you were a true lover. By your home-call, I have found myself in a very serious delirium and who is there again to make herself a bridge of reconciliation to be solving problems among trouble makers? It is our prayers that our Heavenly Father, the creator of the universe may have mercy on you and keep you safely in his celestial kingdom where we hope to meet you during the close of the universal chapter. We love you fondly but your creator’s love beats ours. Fare thee well. Until we meet again. AMEN Read More...
Daniel Kofi Amponsah Larbi TRIBUTE BY WIFE: MRS. KATE LARBI Good people die and no one understands or even cares, but when they die no calamity can hurt them. Those who lived good lives find peace and rest in death. (Isaiah 57:1-2) As a young lady, in the 2000s, I met Father, as I affectionately call him. We became pals and I observed his remarkable intelligence, confidence, knowledge, and charming personality. God has been good to us as a family and you always testified of God's goodness in our lives. Every morning during our prayer time, you never ceased to thank God for His grace, mercy, and favour. You loved the Lord deeply and, whatever you did, you did it as unto the Lord. You spent time studying the bible over and over. In recent years, you took to reading the not-so-popular books of the bible and you enjoyed discussing them. During our Wednesday morning bible studies, you would come up with very deep insights into the passages we read. Our relationship got better with age. So much so that, if the children asked one of us for advice without the other being there, oftentimes our answers would be similar. We shared a closeness that was a source of amusement to our friends and family. It is often said, that there may be no perfect partners since perfection belongs to the Almighty God alone, but an ideal partner can be found in someone who has developed certain ways that go beyond looks, charm, and success: and that was Father. We have known each other for almost two decades, and when you talk about having a real partner, it was evident in our relationship because there was growth and understanding of our traumas and its influence on our comportment. We had our little fights but we settled our issues without the intervention of an outsider. Father was very open and always accepted his vulnerabilities; his openness made him express his feelings, thoughts, dreams, and desires outrightly. He had so much interest in his personal development. Father was very particular about everything around him, a perfectionist, dedicated, committed, result-oriented, and also a much-focused man. Indeed, Father was a good loving husband, and a wonderful father to our children - Leonida, Theonas, Emelia and Macrina.. As a father of our children, their relationship cannot be overestimated and exaggerated, but you will admire father and the children. On that faithful day, 15th March 2022, when the Lord called you home, I was devastated because it was unexpected, but I take solace in the fact that the Lord who took you away from us will continue to be our protector. I will continue to imbibe in our children your legacy. Thank you for all you did to make the world a better place for Us. We love you and miss you. But our children and I are consoled that you are with the Lord. Rest well, my dear, till we meet again Amen. Read More...
